# Code of conduct

L4DStats welcomes constructive participation from people of every background
and experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and patient.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Welcome corrections and acknowledge uncertainty.
- Protect privacy. Never post private telemetry or identify a player for public
  accusation.
- Keep discussion relevant to improving the project.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing,
deliberate intimidation, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when necessary to protect the community. Decisions should be
proportionate, documented privately, and considerate of both impact and
context.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's Security tab. If
private reporting is unavailable, open an issue with no sensitive details and
ask for a private contact channel. Maintainers will handle reports discreetly
and will not knowingly disclose a reporter's identity without permission,
except where required by law or necessary to address an immediate safety risk.
